A standard deviation is a unit of measurement that quantifies certain outcomes relative to the average outcome.
  • 1 standard deviation is about 68.2% of outcomes
  • 2 standard deviation is about 95.4% of outcomes
The standard deviation of implied volatility is the stock price X implied volatility X square root of days to expiration divided by 365. The standard deviation tells us the expected range for a stock price when applied to the current value. For example, for stock XYZ at a price of $100 with a standard deviation of $10 says that the stock is 68.2% likely to be in the range between $90 and $100 on expiration
